About us

Nollies is a place for simplicity. Good food, good beer, and good service. Simple is the name of the game, and we’re good at finding it in all the right places, even in your dining experience. We’re a small group from Carolina Beach, North Carolina who make simple food possible. Come join us and see what simplicity tastes like.

Enjoy Craft Beer!

Craft Beers

Here at Nollies we're all about the love of beer. New and bold flavors enter our doors every week, and we can't help but show them off. While we enjoy the classics, we’re always passionate about discovering something new, so stop by and experience our craft at its best.
